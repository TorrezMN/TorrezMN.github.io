Educacion

In this section we explore the "educational aspect" of the data with special enphasis in the inpact on the IT world of the paraguayan profesionals.


It can be seen that there is a reduced number of Majors represented in the data by the survey respondents.

The majors with the highest numbers of representation in the data are:

  • Programming
  • CS Engineering
  • Systems analysis

More than 50% of the representation in the dataset is distributed among these three majors, with Systems Analysis being the major with the highest representation.
